Sales Intern

Full Time
Remote (2 months / 3 months) Internship (Unpaid)

We are seeking enthusiastic and driven individuals to join us as Sales Interns. This internship is a great opportunity to gain hands-on experience in B2B sales, lead generation, customer engagement, and sales strategy. You will work closely with the founder and sales team to build relationships, understand client needs, and contribute to the expansion of Vegrowpure’s client base in the vegetable market ecosystem

Key Responsibilities:

  • Identify and research potential customers through online platforms and databases.
  • Schedule and participate in sales calls with prospective clients
  • Support ongoing communication with existing clients to maintain relationships and explore upselling opportunities.
  • Assist in preparing proposals, follow-up emails, and presentation decks
  • Record and update sales interactions and lead status using CRM tools.
  • Participate in virtual product demos and help address basic client queries.
  • Provide insights from customer interactions to improve product positioning and marketing efforts.

Requirements:

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Interest in sales, customer success, or business development
  • Basic knowledge of Excel and CRM tools (training can be provided).
  • Ability to stay organized, multitask, and meet deadlines.
  • Confidence, professionalism, and a learning mindset.
  • Self-motivated with a passion for solving problems and creating value for customers.

What You’ll Gain:

  • Potential PPI/PPO based on performance (Upto 4.5 Lpa)
  • Real-world experience in B2B sales, lead management, and client communication.
  • Learn the full sales cycle from prospecting to closing.
  • Opportunity to build a portfolio with sales strategies and client engagement experiences.
  • Certificate of completion and letter of recommendation (based on performance).
  • Mentorship from startup leadership and exposure to Agri-tech industry insights.

Market Research Analyst Intern

Full Time
Remote (Duration: 3 months) Internship (Unpaid)

We’re seeking motivated and curious individuals for the role of Market Research Analyst Intern. This internship will provide you with an opportunity to support the research team in gathering and analysing market and consumer data. You’ll work closely with the founder to interpret key insights, understand vegetable price trends, study consumer behaviour, and contribute to actionable recommendations that influence business decisions.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist in compiling and reviewing vegetable market data from multiple sources (online platforms, government databases, field inputs, etc.).
  • Organize data using Excel, charts, and graphs for internal and external use.
  • Conduct surveys or polls to understand customer preferences and demand fluctuations
  • Study trends in consumer behaviour and help in predicting future market needs.
  • Help draft concise reports summarizing insights and recommendations for the operations and sales teams.
  • Stay updated on industry trends, competitor activities, and market developments.

Requirements:

  • Strong analytical and strategic thinking skills.
  • Ability to understand and communicate complex data in a simple and structured manner
  • Familiarity with Excel; knowledge of Python, Power BI, or data visualization toolsis a plus.
  • Detail-oriented with the ability to multitask and meet deadlines.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Eagerness to learn, collaborate, and explore market research concepts

What You’ll Gain:

  • Potential PPI/PPO based on performance (Upto 6 Lpa)
  • Hands-on experience in conducting and applying market research in a fast-paced, impact-driven environment.
  • Exposure to real market data and trends affecting the vegetable supply chain
  • Opportunity to build a portfolio of work showcasing data analysis, research findings, and presentations.
  • Certificate of completion and letter of recommendation (based on performance).
  • A chance to work with a passionate team committed to sustainable agriculture and innovation.
