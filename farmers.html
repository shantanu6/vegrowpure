Rajesh Patil

Nashik, Maharashtra
Specialty: Tomatoes, Onions, Peppers
Farming since: 1995

Rajesh comes from a long line of farmers spanning three generations. After completing his agriculture degree, he returned to his ancestral farm to implement modern organic farming techniques while preserving traditional knowledge. His tomatoes are known for their exceptional flavor and are grown without any chemical pesticides.

Lakshmi Devi

Mysore, Karnataka
Specialty: Leafy Greens, Herbs
Farming since: 2008

Lakshmi started her organic farm after leaving her corporate job in Bangalore. She specializes in growing nutrient-rich leafy greens and aromatic herbs using permaculture principles. Her farm is a model for sustainable water management and biodiversity conservation in the region.

Patel Brothers

Anand, Gujarat
Specialty: Potatoes, Carrots, Root Vegetables
Farming since: 1980

The Patel brothers, Nikhil and Suresh, manage a 15-acre farm that has been in their family for over four decades. They've pioneered organic root vegetable cultivation in Gujarat, developing special techniques for growing sweeter carrots and more nutritious potatoes without chemical fertilizers.

Gurpreet Singh

Amritsar, Punjab
Specialty: Organic Fruits, Berries
Farming since: 2010

Gurpreet transformed his conventional farm to organic after witnessing the health impacts of chemical farming in his community. He now grows a variety of fruits and berries, and has created a cooperative that helps other farmers in Punjab transition to organic methods.

Anita Deshmukh

Pune, Maharashtra
Specialty: Exotic Vegetables, Microgreens
Farming since: 2015

A former chef turned farmer, Anita brings her culinary expertise to farming. She grows rare and exotic vegetables and microgreens that are sought after by high-end restaurants. Her small but intensive farm demonstrates how even limited space can be highly productive using vertical farming techniques.

Venkatesh Rao

Belgaum, Karnataka
Specialty: Organic Mangoes, Bananas
Farming since: 1990

Venkatesh manages a 25-acre orchard that produces some of the sweetest mangoes and bananas in Karnataka. He uses traditional farming methods passed down through generations, combined with modern organic certification standards. His farm also serves as an educational center for local schools.
