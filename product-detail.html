Product Description

Our fresh tomatoes are grown using organic farming methods without harmful pesticides. They are harvested at the peak of ripeness to ensure maximum flavor and nutritional value.

Tomatoes are an excellent source of vitamin C, potassium, folate, and vitamin K. They are also a good source of the antioxidant lycopene, which has been linked to many health benefits, including reduced risk of heart disease and cancer.

Benefits of Organic Tomatoes

  • Higher in antioxidants compared to conventionally grown tomatoes
  • Free from harmful pesticides and chemicals
  • Better taste and flavor
  • Environmentally friendly farming practices
  • Supports local farmers and sustainable agriculture

How to Store

For maximum freshness, store tomatoes at room temperature away from direct sunlight. Refrigerating tomatoes can cause them to lose flavor and develop a mealy texture. If you need to store them for a longer period, you can refrigerate them, but bring them back to room temperature before consuming for the best flavor.
